KOENIGSEGG

Koenigsegg is a Swedish hypercar manufacturer founded by Christian von Koenigsegg in 1994. The company produces some of the fastest and most technologically advanced cars in the world. Their vehicles are made in very small numbers, often by hand, which makes each model rare and extremely expensive. Koenigsegg is famous for breaking speed records and using unusual engineering solutions, such as unique upward-opening doors and very powerful engines. Their cars combine luxury, innovation, and extreme performance, which is why they are a dream for many car enthusiasts.
