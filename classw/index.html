Jesko - it's about speed

The Koenigsegg Jesko is an extreme high-performance hypercar designed for maximum speed and advanced aerodynamics. It features a lightweight carbon fiber body, a powerful twin-turbo V8 engine, and cutting-edge technology focused on track performance and stability. The Jesko is built in very limited numbers, making it rare and highly exclusive.
