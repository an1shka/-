JESKO ABSOLUTE

The Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut is designed to be the fastest Koenigsegg ever made. It has a highly aerodynamic body with a long tail to reduce drag. The car is powered by a twin-turbo V8 engine producing up to 1,600 horsepower on E85 fuel. The Jesko Absolut focuses on achieving extreme top speed and stability.

GEMERA

The Koenigsegg Gemera is a revolutionary four-seat hypercar introduced in 2020. Unlike most hypercars, it offers space for four passengers while still delivering extreme performance. It combines a hybrid powertrain with a powerful combustion engine. The Gemera represents the future of high-performance cars with both speed and practicality.

AGERA

The Koenigsegg Agera is a Swedish hypercar known for its extreme speed and advanced engineering. It was produced between 2010 and 2018. The Agera features a lightweight carbon fiber body and a powerful twin-turbo V8 engine. It became famous for setting multiple world speed records and showcasing innovative technology.

CCXR

The Koenigsegg CCXR is a high-performance hypercar introduced in 2007. It was one of the first “green” hypercars because it could run on biofuel (E85). The CCXR produces over 1,000 horsepower and combines luxury with incredible speed. It played an important role in Koenigsegg’s global recognition.
